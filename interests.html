My friends & Adventures

Ship Graveyard

Bit of a spooky place filled with rusty old baloons. Lovely people though, this is where I attended my first ever 'Fish are friends' meeting. I'm proud to say that I've never eaten a fish, and I support all my good friends making that choice every day!

Jellyfish Fields

The most fun place to bounce! If you love bouncing, you'll love this place! Be careful though, there was something you shouldn't do here... or something you should? Anyway, happy bouncing!

The E.A.C

The East Australian current is where I met Crush the sea turtle and his very cool family. The turtles are amazing fun to hang out with, they love a good thrill ride!

Sydney Harbour

Sydney is where it all started, I spent many years here at the Aquarium making friends and planning daring escapes! It was also the end of my amazing quest with Marlin to find his son Nemo!
